Wandering Monster

 



1d6 every 3 turns(30 minutes), encounter a monster from the dungeon's table on 1. Wandering monsters that are killed are removed from the table. Wandering monsters usually don't carry a lot of treasure.


Morale

 



-2-
	Surrender

3-5
	Flee

6-8
	Attack! When losing, initiate parlay or standoff

9-11
	Press for advantage!

-12-
	Attack impetuously! (no more morale checks)

Roll on first death in enemy party and once again once half of the party is dead. For single monsters at 60 and 20% HP.


Loyalty

 



-2-
	Refuse outright, -1 loyalty(personal reason?)

3-5
	Refuse

6-7
    Barter/ Negotiate

7-11
	Accept Task

-12-
	Of course! Inspired +1 loyalty!

Roll whenever a PC asks a henchman to do something dangerous or Arduous. Add Loyalty bonus.
Loyalty starts at 0(maybe more if the party helped them before). Loyalty goes up as the henchman travels with the party. It goes down whenever a task they attempt goes very badly or the party is incompetent.

Henchman leaves at -4 loyalty.

 No attack, monster leaves

-12-
 Enthusiastic friendship


Reaction II: Advanced

 



1

Charge! Monsters attack. They want to eat the PCs, take all their stuff and/or sell them as slaves. If the monsters know that they are clearly inferior, they will flee instead.

2

Ambush! Monsters attack with stealth and/or trickery. Goblins will disguise themselves as children (badly), a bear might hide in the cupboard. Treat this as 1 for dumb monsters.

3

Hit and run! Monsters attack, then they gtfo unless they have great initial success. They might try to grab something/someone.

4

Show of dominance: Monsters beat their drums, wave their spears and/or sprinkle the PCs with urine. Monsters wants the PCs to kowtow, give them treasure and/or do their bidding.

5

Defend territory: Monsters makes it clear that this room/corridor is theirs and that the PCs should go another way. Bribery and/or flattery might work.

6

Nature: Monster does something typical for its nature. Orcs charge without much tactics, trolls ask riddles, wizards meddle in the affairs of mortals.

7

Trade: Monsters have X, wants to trade it for something the PCs have (food, gold, henchmen, etc.). Dumb monsters might make stupid trades. Proud monsters will take refusal to trade as an insult.

8

Quest: Monsters needs the PCs to find something or fight someone. Monsters will offer help and/or a reward, but might not pay when push comes to show. Powerful monsters will use geas.

-2-

Putter around, do things you did in life! mumble to nobody, smash things, move stuff around.

3-5

Play dead. Ghosts hide in walls. Attempt sneak attack.

6-8

Stare blankly into space. Attack if PCs come close.

9-11

Attack!

-12-

(Temporarily)Lucid. Attempt to communicate with PCs. (ask for food, news, loved ones, what happened to me?)
