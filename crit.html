Crit/ Fumble Tables




Introduction

In DCC, when you make a critical hit in melee combat, you roll on a Crit table. What effects are available depend on 1: your crit die(goes up by level) and 2:Your crit table. Classes have different crit tables, the fighter and Dwarf get access to better crit tables as they gain levels. All crit rolls are modified by luck, higher is generally better.

Example: All level 0 character roll on crit table 1. They have a 1d4 crit die, so a character with a luck modifier of 0 can only get effects 1-4 on crit table 1. If you roll a natural 1 you roll on the Fumble table, the die you roll depends on your armor.
